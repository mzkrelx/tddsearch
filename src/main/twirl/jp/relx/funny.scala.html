@() Twirl makes some special decisions in rendering certain types, including:
  • Collections like
    • List: @List("first", "second", "third")
    • Seq: @Seq("first", "second", "third")
  • Option
    • Some: @Some("foo")
    • None: @None
  • But not Either
    • Left: @Left("foo")
    • Right: @Right("bar")
  • Others?
